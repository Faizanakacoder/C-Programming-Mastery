# Bitwise Operators in C - Quick Notes

## Definition

Bitwise operators work on binary numbers.

---

## Operators

| Operator | Meaning |
|-----------|----------|
| & | AND |
| \| | OR |
| ^ | XOR |
| ~ | NOT |
| << | Left Shift |
| >> | Right Shift |

---

## Examples

```c
a & b
a | b
a ^ b
~a
a << 1
a >> 1
```

---

## Important Point

Bitwise operators work on **binary representation** of numbers.

---

## Short Summary

Bitwise operators perform operations at bit level.
